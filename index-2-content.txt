--- EXPERTISE SHRINES ---

Expertise

01  Systems Architecture
I architect digital products as cohesive ecosystems rather than isolated screens.
By establishing a consistent "logic" for the interface—grounded in user mental
models and technical constraints—I build scalable UI systems that transform
high-density data into intuitive, navigable landscapes.

02  AI-Augmented Product Thinking
I leverage AI as a strategic partner to accelerate product evolution and solve
business-critical challenges. From using AI-driven behavioral mapping to identify
"retention leaks" to integrating AI-powered content into product roadmaps, I
implement high-impact interventions that drive long-term engagement.

03  Information Architecture (留白)
I manage information density through the lens of Strategic Restraint. By applying
the principle of 留白 (Liú Bái), I balance technical precision with intentional
minimalism to reduce cognitive load—ensuring that the user's expertise and
"tribal knowledge" remain the primary focus of the experience.
